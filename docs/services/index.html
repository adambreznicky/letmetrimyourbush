Local Yard Work: Services

Mowing

One time or routine visits, just let me know what works best for you. Going out of town and need the lawn mowed just once or twice for the time you're away? Just let me know. When it comes to routine, I can accommodate your preference. I currently serve clients on weekly, bi-weekly, and even rigid monthly date schedules.

Trimming

Mowing the grass always demands those sharp edges to really make your lawn stand out. But what about those hedges that are out of hand? I trim bushes and shrubs to keep the entire landscape in line and looking immaculate.

Weeding

Just a couple weeds coming up in the flower beds? I pull them. Weeds trying to reclaim the stone walkway or bed? I spray them. Let me keep those weeds where they belong: Gone!

Mulching

Mulch is necessity to keep the flower beds both nutritious and looking good. I can lay down just a couple bags or fill a truck bed full in bulk.

Leaves & Limbs

A service reserved for routine clients, I can bag and remove those seasonal leaves and small limbs.
